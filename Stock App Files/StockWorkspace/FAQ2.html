Navigating the Individual Stock Page

In our Application, we have a search for stock function that allow the user to search for specific stock fetures. After selecting the correct stock from the dropdown menu, the user is given the choice to either buy the stock or add the stock to their watchlist. The interactive graphs give up to date information on the companies stock prices

Company information

Our application displays information about the company searched such as their name, a brief summary of the company, as well as the stock prices in several time ranges, from live that updates every minute to 5 years back.


Stats

The Stats section contains information on the companies highs and lows,dividend rates,their latest EPS,their debt, and their gross profit.


News

This section presents the user with a link to recent news regarding the Stock Market.
